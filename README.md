# Brazil Top Two Cities Distance (2025)

The two most populous cities in Brazil are generally recognized as:

1. São Paulo
2. Rio de Janeiro

Using Google Maps driving distance:

- Route: São Paulo, SP, Brazil → Rio de Janeiro, RJ, Brazil
- Distance: 435 km
- Approximate driving time: 5 hours 42 minutes

Comparison with the United States:

- New York, NY, USA → Los Angeles, CA, USA: 4,489 km
- São Paulo, SP, Brazil → Rio de Janeiro, RJ, Brazil: 435 km

Conclusion:

The distance between the two most populous cities in the USA is higher than the distance between the two most populous cities in Brazil.
